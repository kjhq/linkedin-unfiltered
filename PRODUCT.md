# Product

## Register

product

## Users

General professionals who use LinkedIn casually and find it performative. They scroll their feed, see humble-brags and engagement bait, and want someone to cut through it. They aren't power users optimizing their workflow; they're people who want the entertainment of seeing LinkedIn posts translated into honest, blunt text. Their context is casual browsing, not productivity.

## Product Purpose

LinkedIn Unfiltered translates LinkedIn posts into honest, no-BS versions using AI. The core value is amusement: seeing performative professional content reframed through satire and blunt honesty. The extension should feel like a clever friend whispering the real translation next to you while you scroll.

## Brand Personality

Witty, irreverent, sharp. The voice is the friend who says what everyone's thinking. Not mean-spirited, not cynical, but playfully honest. Three words: clever, blunt, fun.

## Anti-references

- Corporate SaaS dashboards with blue gradients, glassmorphism, and glass cards
- Chrome Web Store generic extension popups with cramped settings and plain forms
- Anything that feels like enterprise software or productivity tools
- Over-designed interfaces that take themselves too seriously

## Design Principles

1. The humor is the product; the UI should get out of its way
2. Polish signals quality; even a joke extension should feel intentional
3. The UI should be invisible until it delivers the punchline
4. Every interaction should be fast; waiting kills comedy timing

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard browser extension accessibility: keyboard navigable popup, sufficient contrast on all text, screen reader support for the popup controls. The content script injects results inline, so it inherits LinkedIn's own accessibility patterns.
